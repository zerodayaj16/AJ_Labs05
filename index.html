Watchlists & Intel

Paste your own known-bad indicators and optional IP → country/ASN mapping. Everything here stays in this browser tab.

Watchlist — IPs & domains (one per line)

IP Intel — CSV: ip,country,asn

Watchlist matches add a CRITICAL "Watchlist hit" correlation. IP intel enables the impossible-travel correlation (a user seen from 2+ distinct countries within 4 hours) and annotates IPs in SOC Tools. Loading, scoring, and correlating logs here never makes a network call — the only feature in this app that does is the optional Live Lookup in the Reputation view, and only when you click it.

Reputation

Every IP, domain, and URL in your logs, scored offline by heuristics — with optional live lookups against public threat-intel APIs.

Live Lookup settings (optional)

Keys are used only to call VirusTotal/AbuseIPDB directly from your browser when you click a lookup button — they're never sent anywhere else, never included in exported sessions/reports, and (unless you check "remember") are cleared on reload. URLhaus needs no key. Some providers block direct browser requests (CORS); if a lookup fails immediately, that's the likely cause — their own website will still work.

Check your own indicators

Paste any mix of IPs, domains, and URLs — one per line. Type is auto-detected. These are scored the same way as indicators from your logs, and work even with no logs loaded.
